US Electric Power Generation

Biomass, Geothermal, and Solar Comparison

Map authored by Anna Harrington

This map displays power generation capacity from renewable energy sources across the United States. Click on any symbol to view plant details.

What principle did you use? Shape differentiation to reduce visual clutter.

What change did you make? Each energy type uses a distinct shape (circles for Biomass, triangles for Geothermal, and squares for Solar) rather than the previous identical circles.

Why do you think the map has a better user experience? This change makes overlapping points easier to view and distinguish, allowing viewers to quickly more easily identify energy types in dense regions without relying on color alone. This change can improve both clarity and accessibility.
